Salam and Greetings!

Born in 2004 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, I've pursued a robust academic journey, focusing on pure sciences at SMK Seri Bintang Utara and earning a Diploma in Chemical Engineering from UiTM Johor, Pasir Gudang Campus. As a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador, I aim to empower my youth community by promoting the use of essential Microsoft tools like Excel and cutting-edge technologies such as Copilot. In addition to my ambassadorial role, I hold certifications as a Python programmer and enterprise data practitioner, and I've also been recognized for my skills in data labeling, contributing to the future of artificial intelligence. Notably, my contributions in the STEM field were acknowledged when I was named a 2021 School STEM Icon and won multiple awards for innovation, including accolades from the British International Education Association (BIEA).

With a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of engineering and technology, I'm well-equipped to offer my skills in various domains. My experience as a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador has honed my ability to teach and inspire others, while my certifications demonstrate a solid grasp of programming and data practices. Having received numerous awards for innovation, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise that can drive forward-thinking projects and initiatives within your company.

Evaluating Nanoparticle Suspension Stability in Nanofluids: The Roles of Ultrasonic Treatment and Surfactant Addition via UV-Vis Spectroscopy and FTIR Analysis.

In this study, we investigate the stability of nanoparticle suspensions in nanofluids, focusing on the impact of ultrasonic treatment and surfactant addition. Utilizing UV-Vis spectroscopy and FTIR analysis, we aim to provide a comprehensive understanding of how these factors influence nanoparticle dispersion and stability. Additionally, we explore the use of biosurfactants as an alternative to traditional surfactants to optimize stability. This research offers valuable insights for the development and optimization of nanofluid applications.
